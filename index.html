Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right career path?

Consider your interests, strengths, and market demand. Use our Adventure Mode for younger students or Sector Mode for focused exploration. Youvah AI can also provide personalized guidance.

What is the best way to prepare for UPSC?

Start with NCERTs, follow a disciplined routine, read newspapers daily, and practice answer writing. Consistency over 1-2 years is key. Check our detailed IAS roadmap for step-by-step guidance.

Are there careers outside of Engineering and Medicine?

Absolutely! India offers diverse opportunities in Design, Psychology, Data Science, Defense, Civil Services, Arts, and traditional crafts. Explore our Arts and Creative section for unconventional paths.

How accurate is the salary information?

Our salary data is compiled from government pay commissions, industry reports, and professional surveys. Government salaries follow 7th Pay Commission standards, while private sector ranges reflect market averages.
